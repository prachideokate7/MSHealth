Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will my data remain secure?

    This system is end-to-end encrypted. No one can view your documents without your consent. Patients have all rights to share their data only with people whom they want to share. Patients can give data access to health facilitators and withdraw immediately if they want to.

  • Can I handle my family member’s account who is unable to handle it?

    Yes, you can handle your family members/colleague’s accounts with his consent. For that, we have a nominee account system where you can create a nominee account and can handle his account.

  • Can I upload my previous health records?

    Yes, you can upload your previous medical records. You will have two options to digitalize your previous data. First is, you can scan your previous records at hospital using our digital scanner and can convert previous records. Second is, you can directly upload your previous documents from your mobile.

  • Should I keep offline documents?

    No, once you upload your documents on our app, you are free to keep offline records away. Now, you can access your data anytime, anywhere without any concern to carry it out.

  • Can I share my health-related photos and videos on health media?

    Yes, you can upload your photos and videos on our health media. In health media, you will find imp links, files, videos, and photos shared by doctors, hospitals, and fitness mentors. You can like, comment, and follow someone who can make more influence on your daily health routine and health awareness.

  • How can I send my medical documents to doctors and hospitals?

    Yes, we have given you all the rights and facilities to handle your data how you want to handle it. We have given you access options, using that you can give access to hospitals and doctors to see your medical records.

Importance of Instant Consultation

Nowadays, taking time out to visit doctor can be difficult because of our busy schedules. Travelling long distance to a doctor, waiting for long hours in lines seems too time consuming for people who are on tight schedule. That is why most of time, people ignore their health problems. Realizing you have health issues and still ignoring symptoms, people are hampering their health and healthy life. It is necessary to discuss your problems with doctors. So you will get proper advice to solve your health issues in early stages. After consultation, regarding to your body symptoms you will get proper guidance about your condition and suggestions from the experts in particular condition. Now instant consultation is easy and convenient to all with online mode via video call with doctors. There are no location boundaries, so it is beneficial in various ways.

  1. You will get your issues solved from experts you want.
  2. Not needed to travel long distance.
  3. You can save your time and money.

You will get prescription and proper advice and especially beneficial for geriatric population also. Some conditions where patient is immobile, you can consult from your home makes comfortable and convenient to all. People living in remote area, availability of doctors is difficult. Consulting with doctors online will eliminate travel for long distance as people living in remote areas have to travel for city which is time consuming. Online consultation platforms provide opportunities to all of us to make our life healthy. Let’s not stuck in the fixed mindset to accept emerging technology, use it and make life better.
